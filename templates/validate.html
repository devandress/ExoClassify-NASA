About NEOSSat

NEOSSat (Near Earth Object Surveillance Satellite) is a Canadian microsatellite designed to search for asteroids and satellites that could contaminate astronomical observations, including exoplanet studies.
When you enter your observation coordinates, NEOSSat simulates the sky conditions for your field of view and date. It analyzes the risk of contamination from bright stars, the Moon, and possible asteroid or satellite crossings.
The system estimates the contamination level and provides recommendations to improve your observation quality.

Orbit
~800 km
Purpose
Asteroid & Satellite Detection
